# Store Listing

## Description

Nortid tells the time in spelled-out Scandinavian words, like "halv tre" and "kvart på fem", in Norwegian, Danish, or Swedish, with an optional date line below. Add up to three health stats at the top (steps, heart rate, sleep), or switch the time to plain numbers in 12- or 24-hour format. Colors are fully customizable, and it runs on every Pebble model. Simple, readable, and quietly elegant.
